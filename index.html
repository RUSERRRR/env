About Envision

We are the Visionaries.

Envision is a global consultancy network that offers insights on real-life issues and companies, bringing together bright minds worldwide to make a positive impact. We bridge emerging talent and the professional world.

Collaboration and inclusivity drive our success as we nurture talent, encourage personal growth, and uphold our core values of integrity, excellence, and innovation. Our platform offers in-depth consultancy reports on relevant topics and partnerships.

Readers gain insights from our in-depth reports or thought-provoking articles put together by our ambitious team — together, we are building a brighter future, one visionary project at a time.

Phase 1 ▸Currently active — Developing online presence, building a network of ambitious contributors, and publishing high-quality reports.
Phase 2Regional chapters worldwide, video and podcast content featuring practitioners and market leaders.
Phase 3In-person contributor summits and a fully-realised global professional network spanning industries and institutions.

      Build Your Career Here.

      Three distinct paths — each designed to help you grow as a consultant, editor, or leader.

      01 // Consultant

      Consultant

      Join a specialised team and contribute to real consultancy challenges affecting real organisations.

      • Work within a specialised sector team
      • Write high-value consultancy reports
      • Analyse challenges and provide solutions
      • Conduct thorough research
      • Grow under the mentorship of experienced board leads
      Apply as Consultant

      02 // Editor

      Editor

      The invisible force behind every great publication — ensure the utmost quality across all Envision reports.

      • Ensure clarity and coherence of reports
      • Correct errors and maintain style standards
      • Provide feedback to contributors and team leads
      • Optimise content for digital reach
      • Work across multiple team specialisations
      Apply as Editor

      03 // Board

      Board Lead

      Lead and empower your team with visionary guidance. The most senior contributor role at Envision.

      • Lead a specialised team with full authority
      • Oversee quality for accuracy and professionalism
      • Attend strategic board meetings
      • Shape Envision future direction
      • Requires letter of motivation
      Apply for Board
